## Who Is Whogohost?

Whogohost has been around since 2007 — started by Opeyemi Awoyemi when he was still a student at OAU, originally as a side to his web design work. Toba Obaniyi joined in 2010, the company was formally incorporated in 2011, and somewhere along the line it became the biggest web hosting company in West Africa.

Today they serve over 40,000 businesses and individuals across Nigeria and the wider continent. They hold accreditations from IBM, InterSwitch, Google (as a Workspace reseller), and Cloudflare. NIRA voted them No.1 Domain Registrar in Nigeria. They've even been recognised as Best ccTLD Registrar in 2025. Not bad for something that started in a university hostel.

The company recently rebranded its platform to GO54, but the Whogohost name and website remain active — and for Nigerians, both names refer to the same service.
